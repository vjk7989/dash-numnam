# Product

## Register

product

## Users

NumNam super administrators and support operators who monitor app operations, manage content, review user activity, moderate community spaces, and handle privacy-sensitive health workflows.

## Product Purpose

Provide one clear operational workspace for understanding NumNam activity and acting on users, content, notifications, reports, moderation, and system health. Success means administrators can find important data quickly and complete routine actions without losing context.

## Brand Personality

Warm, trustworthy, and capable.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, decorative card walls, novelty controls, overly dark enterprise styling, and color used without operational meaning.

## Design Principles

- Put operational clarity before decoration.
- Keep common actions and status visible at a glance.
- Use the friendly NumNam identity without reducing professional trust.
- Protect sensitive health data with explicit context and reason gates.
- Preserve familiar admin patterns and responsive keyboard-accessible controls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, semantic navigation and tables, color-independent status labels, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion support.
